AUGMIX: A SIMPLE DATA PROCESSING METHOD TOIMPROVE ROBUSTNESS AND UNCERTAINTY

发表于2022-12-04|Paper Notes

Introduction

Training against corruptions only encourages networks to memorize the specific corruptions seen during training and leaves models unable to generalize to new corruptions

Related work

  • Robustness under Data shift

    training against distortions can often fail to generalize to unseen distortions, as networks have a tendency to memorize properties of the specific training distortion

    training with various blur augmentations can fail to generalize to unseen blurs or blurs with different parameter settings

  • Calibration under Data shift

    metrics for determining the calibration of machine learning models

    simply ensembling classifier predictions improves prediction calibration

    pre-training can also improve calibration

    model calibration substantially deteriorates under data shift

  • Data Augmentation

    A popular way to make networks robust to lpl_plp​ adversarial examples is with adversarial training, which we use in this paper.

AugMix

先前的方案将augmentation组合成链状，容易导致image degrade and drift off the data mainfold


  • Augmentation

    AugMix用到的operation和ImageNet-C是不相交的

    随机选择kkk条augmentation chains, 每条chain上compose 1~3种augmentation

  • Mixing

    使用elementwise convex combination

  • Jensen-Shannon Divergence Consistency Loss

    假设现在有porig,paugmix1,paugmix2p_{orig},p_{augmix1},p_{augmix2}porig​,paugmix1​,paugmix2​，这个散度可以理解为测量imformation about the identity of the distributions的均值

Experiment

    Dataset Usage
    CIFAR-10-C, CIFAR100-C, ImageNet-C to measure a model’s resilience to data shift 每种corruption有5个severities
    CIFAR-10-P, CIFAR-100-P, ImageNet-P these datasets contain smaller corruptions and are used to measure the classifier’s prediction stability
    Each example in these datasets is a video
